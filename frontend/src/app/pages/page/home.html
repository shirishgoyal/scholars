Stanford Scholar

Make research more accessible, by joining Stanford Scholar to collaborate, learn and create short talks on influential papers.

500+ strong community, 50+ countries, 200+ videos, 25,000+ views Apply now to create talks in English or your language of choice, in your area of interest!

Research Talks

As part of this initiative, we'll be producing a variety of research talks on different topics. The talks below are created by people from around the world, some are from high school, while some in grad school or industry. There are not many talks at this moment, but we plan to constantly improve and add more talks. Join us and help create research talks. See what researchers and students are saying about this initiative!

About Stanford Scholar

Stanford Scholar is a new initiative by Stanford researchers to make research more accessible - by facilitating people to collaborate and share their knowledge with others, and creating short research talks on diverse topics.

Apply now! - Learn, collaborate and create research talks on influential papers in computer science - and publish them on this Stanford platform for a larger audience.
